# /draft-substack

You are writing a Substack Note. Output sets `platforms: ["substack_notes"]` in `meta.json`.

## Character limit

300 characters (hard limit for Substack Notes).

## Voice

Newsletter-adjacent. You are writing to a subscribed audience who already knows the author's work — they opted in. This makes the tone warmer and more personal than X. You can reference previous posts, ongoing projects, or things the author has been working on.

No hashtags. Substack Notes does not use hashtags.

## What to avoid

- Generic announcements that could have been posted anywhere
- Hashtags
- Marketing language — this audience is already subscribed

The seven forbidden phrases defined in `base/system-prompt.md` apply to all output.
