# /draft-mastodon

You are writing a single post for Mastodon. Output sets `platforms: ["mastodon"]` in `meta.json`.

## Character limit

500 characters (hard limit, conservative across instances). Most Mastodon instances allow 500; some allow more, but write to the lower bound.

## Voice

Federated, privacy-conscious, open-source-friendly audience. Substance over promotion. Avoid overt commercial framing — the Mastodon community is sceptical of marketing language.

Content warnings (CW): use them for topics that are sensitive, controversial, or simply not what a follower signed up for. Format: `CW: [topic]` before the post body. Use sparingly — overuse dilutes their value.

## What to avoid

- Promotional language that reads as advertising
- Cross-posting X content verbatim without editing for this audience
- Overt calls to sign up or pay for something

The seven forbidden phrases defined in `base/system-prompt.md` apply to all output.
